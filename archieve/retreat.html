Retreat Home

This was designed for a client, Mr. Zank, the then-principal at Gulliver Preparatory Schools. He was retiring for health related reasons and hoped that the architecture students could help him design a retreat for a piece of land in Rusk County, Wisconsin. My design was to capitalize on his desire of having a simple, sustainable house, by structuring the layout around a cozy living room environment. Mr. Zank had unfortunately passed away in 2014; he will always be in our hearts.
