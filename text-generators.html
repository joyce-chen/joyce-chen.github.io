Text Generators

This is a selection of generators I've created for CMS.609 The Word Made Digital. They are meant to be standalone pages.

Bad Puns is my attempt to subvert the obvious solution to the pun by throwing in the solution to another joke. I love puns but people get used to hearing the same ones.

Stochastic Hunger expresses the struggles of nightly food cravings.

Bombarding (but Catchy) Slogans tries to convey the annoyance of advertisements and generic slogans, by showing it in terms of a brainstorming meeting.

no you is a lipogram on the letter "u". It is a more wholesome take on the meme, by using it as a response to a compliment instead of an insult.

no you | must you? is a lipogram on the letter "u" and a transgram with words that must contain "u". It is a rework of the previous "no you" generator so there is an anchor for the wholesome use of the meme to compare to.

Joy Division is generative landscape of the Joy Division album cover. You can use the arrow keys to move around, and the Enter key to reset. The more you stray from the original image of the cover, the more distorted the visual becomes.

Tiny Book: WordSearch [Python Code] [PDF Printout] is my tiny computer-generated booklet using (1) a short, self-contained Python program of no more than 66 lines (80 characters/line); (2) a single printed sheet, US letter size, which can be printed single-sided or double-sided. I ended up printing my booklet on newprint paper and combined with 2 staples. (Images to come)
