About

Joyce is a consumer (and sometimes creator) of all types of media from traditional pencil drawings to CADs to animation to comic books to video games...

She is a low-key self-proclaimed guru of indie rogue-likes, but occasionally hypes about other triple-A titles such as Dark Souls (and Bloodborne).

Currently, she is in her sophomore year at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), studying to be a EECS major (6-2) with a concentration in Comparative Media Studies (CMS).



In another part of her life, Joyce programs, designs, and builds cool things, but these contraptions have yet to been included on this website! (UROP and engineering stuff)



The only thing she likes more than sarcasm is writing about herself in the third person.
